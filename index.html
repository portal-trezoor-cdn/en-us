Get started with your new Trézor

Trézor.io is the original hardware wallet designed to secure your cryptocurrency assets. With Trézor, you can safely store your Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other cryptocurrencies.

Getting Started with Trézor.io

Welcome to Trézor.io/start! Here, we will guide you through the process of setting up your Trézor hardware wallet. Follow these steps to ensure your crypto assets are secure:

  • Unbox your Trézor device and connect it to your computer using the provided USB cable.
  • Visit the official Trézor website and download the Trézor Bridge software.
  • Install the Trézor Bridge software and follow the on-screen instructions to set up your device.
  • Create a new wallet or recover an existing one using your recovery seed.
  • Set a strong PIN to protect your device from unauthorized access.
  • Write down your recovery seed and store it in a safe place. This seed is crucial for recovering your wallet if your device is lost or damaged.
  • Once your wallet is set up, you can start transferring your crypto assets to your Trézor device.
  • Use the Trézor Wallet interface to manage your assets, send and receive cryptocurrencies, and monitor your portfolio.

Trézor.io/start is designed to make the setup process as smooth as possible. If you encounter any issues, our support team is here to help. Secure your crypto assets with Trézor and enjoy peace of mind knowing your investments are safe.
